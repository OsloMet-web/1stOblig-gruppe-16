This assignment counts as 1 of the 8 possible obligatory assignments in this course. Remember that you have to pass 5 of them to qualify for the exam. Assignment 0, on group work, is not optional, but must be completed by all groups.

Working in Groups

  1. Download the attached document Download documentcontaining the assignment and the assignment form.
  2. Save the document. Include your group number in the title of the file.
  3. Familiarize yourself with the assignment and assignment form before your group discussions in the lab
  4. Read the assignment instructions in the document. Use the assignment form in the document to guide your discussions. Fill in the form as your discussions progress. Save as Word file.
  5. Only one member of the group needs to submit in Canvas.

The whole assignment should be done in the assignment form. Please do not submit in any other format. The document contains instructions for how to proceed. Please read these carefully.
